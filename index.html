About Me

I am a scientist at the Vikram Sarabhai Space Center, Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), where I work on space mechanisms and robotics. My research interests lie at the intersection of optimal control theory, optimization, and machine learning. More broadly, I am driven by the research on controlling the dynamical systems with uncertainties. I completed my bachelors in aerospace engineering from the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST). During my time at IIST, I was fortunate to collaborate with Dr. Pankaj Priyadarshi and Dr. Aravind Vaidyanathan for a thesis-based project and internship.

This portfolio showcases my academic journey, research contributions, and publications, demonstrating my commitment to rigorous inquiry and innovation. Feel free to explore my projects and connect with me!
